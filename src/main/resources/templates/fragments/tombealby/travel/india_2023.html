India 2023
From Kolkata to the Himalayas

In December 2023 we flew to India to visit family and friends. While there, we took a seven day road trip from Kolkata to Darjeeling. This page contains highlights of that trip.

Its about four hundred miles from Kolkata to Darjeeling. We broke the journey up by staying at Berhampore, Malda and Siliguri along the way.

It was fascinating to see the West Bengal countryside on the drive. The following photos show rice being grown in farmland at Malda.

The distance from Siliguri to Darjeeling is about forty miles. However the road is winding and quite narrow. There are steep drops off the road side. Therefore care is needed and our drive took several hours. However it was a clear fine day and we enjoyed some spectacular views along the way.

We took a break at a roadside cafe on the drive to Darjeeling. The following video shows the view from the cafe.

Its not possible to see Everest from Darjeeling. However we could see Kangchenjunga, which is the third highest mountain in the world. If you look carefully in the following photo taken from Darjeeling, you can see the peaks of the Himalayas touching the clouds.

In Darjeeling we visited a tea garden and saw tea being grown. This was a popular tourist spot. Some of the tourists hired traditional tea pickers costumes to have their photos taken.
